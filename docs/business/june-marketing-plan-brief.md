# June Marketing Plan Brief

## Positioning

Dream Wheels AI helps car owners preview wheels on their own car before buying.

Primary message:

> Try wheels on your own car before you buy them.

Secondary message:

> Upload a car photo and a wheel photo. Get a realistic preview in minutes.

## Scope

Focus only on B2C Telegram Mini App launch.

Do not include:

- B2B widget.
- Dealer website.
- Shop dashboard.
- White-label integrations.

## Target Audience Hypotheses

### Segment 1 — Active Wheel Buyer

User is already choosing wheels and needs confidence before purchase.

Likely channels:

- Telegram auto communities.
- VK auto groups.
- Drive2.
- Wheel/tuning discussions.

Primary offer:

- Start package or Founder Pass.

### Segment 2 — Auto Enthusiast

User enjoys car styling and experiments with visual tuning.

Likely channels:

- Telegram/VK tuning groups.
- Drive2 project pages.
- Personal social sharing.

Primary offer:

- Free trial, then small package.

### Segment 3 — Visual Content Sharer

User wants before/after images for posts, polls, or feedback.

Likely channels:

- Telegram chats.
- VK posts.
- Personal Instagram/short video content where available.

Primary offer:

- Free trial with share CTA.

## Required Campaign Output

Codex Cloud should produce:

1. June campaign phases.
2. Channel plan.
3. Daily/weekly execution checklist.
4. Offer copy.
5. Telegram/VK/Drive2 post templates.
6. Free trial funnel.
7. Founder Pass funnel.
8. Metrics dashboard.
9. Budget and free-render subsidy model.
10. Launch risks and mitigation.

## Metrics

Track:

- Mini App opens.
- Upload starts.
- Successful first render.
- Free trial completions.
- Checkout opens.
- Paid conversions.
- Cost per successful render.
- Refund requests.
- Failed generation rate.
- Shares/referrals.
